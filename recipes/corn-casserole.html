Corn casserole

It is so delicious Oh! It is my favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 1/2 large green bell pepper, diced
  • 1(4 ouce) jar chopped pimento peppers
  • 2 eggs
  • 1(8 ounce) package corn muffin mix
  • 1(4 ouce) can diced green chiles
  • 1(15.2 ounce) can whole kernelcorn, with liquid
  • 1(15.2 ounce) can creamed corn
  • 1 pint sour cream
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C). Grease a 9x12-inch baking pan.

  2. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add onion, bell pepper, and pimento peppers and saute until tender, about 5 minutes.

  3. Combine eggs, corn muffin mix, green chiles, whole corn and cream corn in a large bowl with onion mixture. Stir together and pour into the prepared baking pan. Drop sour cream by the spoonful on top of the mixture; cover with cheddar cheese.

  4. Bake in the pre-heated oven until casserole is firm, about 45 minutes.

Note: Pick green chile peppers according to your spice preference(mild, medium, or hot).
