Grilled Cheese

This Sandwich is so good, too delicious that you can not forgive your self eating it.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup freshly grated Parmingiano-Reggiano cheese
  • 8 slices cooked bacon
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • 8 slices sourdough bread

Steps

  1. In a small bowel, mash together the butter and Parmesan cheese until evenly blended. Make Sandwiches by placing two slices of bacon and one slice of Cheddar cheese on half of the bread slices. Top with the remaining slices of the bread.

  2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Spread some of the butter mixture on the top of each sandwich. Place sandwiches butter side down in the skillet. Spread the remaining butter mixture on the other sides. Cook until golden brown on each side, about three minutes per side. To serve, cut in half diagonally.
