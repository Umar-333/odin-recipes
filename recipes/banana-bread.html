Banana bread

It is moist and delicious with banana flavour.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose floor
  • 1 tea spoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 21/3 cups smashed over-ripe bananas

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x12-inch loaf pan.

  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt. In a seperate bowl, cream together butter and brown sugar. Stir in eggs and smashed bananas until well blended. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture; Stir just to moisten. Pour butter into prepared loaf pan.

  3. Bake in pre-heated oven for 60 to 65 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out into wire rack.
